Story 3: The Cautious AI — Safety by Design

In Polish (lowest training data), the model refuses to answer 18% of the time — rather than hallucinating

⚠️ Finding: Polish Refusal Rate = 18.1% vs Chinese 5.8% and Hindi 5.9%. The model retrieves the correct paragraph (Recall@3 = 94.9%) but declines to generate an answer. For legal technology serving vulnerable populations, a conservative AI is a safe AI — refusing is better than hallucinating.
