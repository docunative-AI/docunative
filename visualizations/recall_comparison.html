DocuNative Research Finding

Story 1: The Language Match Discovery

Native-language questions unlock near-perfect retrieval — Recall@3 jumps from 39–66% to 94–99%

🔍 Finding: When users ask in the same language as the document, BGE-M3's retrieval accuracy jumps to near-perfect (94–99%). Cross-lingual retrieval leaves 35–60% of relevant paragraphs unfound. Future direction: translate the user's question into the document language before retrieval — entirely offline.

Recall@3: proportion of queries where the retriever found the correct document paragraph
