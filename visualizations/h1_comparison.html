Story 4: Specialist vs Generalist on Hindi (H1)

Tiny Aya Fire (5.8% Hindi training) vs Tiny Aya Global on Hindi legal documents

🔥 Finding: Fire's Exact Match score is 27% higher than Global's (12.2% vs 9.6%) and its refusal rate is lower (4.4% vs 6.4%), showing greater precision and confidence on Hindi documents. Regional specialisation improves answer precision and model confidence, even without a large F1 advantage.

Both models evaluated on 1,200 Hindi QA pairs. Recall@3 identical (0.444) for both — difference is in generation only.
